UPGRADING LIFESTYLES VIA SMARTER DEVICES

Automate your current ecosystem

SmartiQo’s team is working on a primary aim to “algorithmize” your place’s electrical system for delivering you the freedom to leave your place without thinking about ‘what would be happening over there?’ SmartiQo’s products offer you the comfort of carrying your place, virtually with yourself. The company’s philosophy is to sanction you the relief to live a worry-free life with the help of innovative technology-driven products. SmartiQo dares to imagine a world wherein technology is helping the human species to worry only for the matters in which worrying is actually needed! Pondering upon the idea of reinterpreting the world, SmartiQo believes that micro-automating in the current way of living leads to macro-level impacts on human life.

BRINGING HAPPINESS BY ENSURING SAFETY

Make your Home, Smart Home

24*7 monitoring of appliances is possible using SmartiQo’s smart products. These products give you a complete idea of places from which anyone can enter your place. They are equipped with motion sensors, to detect anyone’s presence. Moreover, the security of the complete system is very tight. By giving you complete assurance of the security at your place, SmartiQo wants to deliver the lost happiness on your face and worry-free life.

SELF-REPORTING GADGETS

Programmed to deal with any situation

SmartiQo’s smart gadgets are not only elegant looking but equally proficient. They are programmed using the latest IFTTT technology. They report to you about the electricity consumption of various appliances at your place. Additionally, they can be controlled from anywhere using your smartphone. Overall, these devices aim to provide you a control switch to deal with any situation at your place, without being physically present at your place.
